26010749 (jckmnhn 2024-09-19 17:28:37 -0400 6) The `ImagePrototype` view would require a banner image for each major, but these could easily be found on UTK's website under their major's descriptions. Sometimes, a large amount of text on a page can be overwhelming, but adding an image or color (such as the capsule shape around the # of required course hours) can help balance any sense of being overwhelmed. Additionally, another fun use of color may include setting the capsule shape to different colors based on the number of additional credit hours required. For example, it could be shades of red for higher numbers indicating caution.
